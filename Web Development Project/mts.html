Medical Officer Dr. David H. Hallock

Dr. Hallock served in the United States Army from 1863 - 1971. With over 100 years of experience serving our country, he has a wide-range of experience in all areas of absurd medical conditions. He graduated from Johns Hopkins Medical School in 1897. Since then, he travelled the world to discover new and bizarre afflictions until settling down in Albert, Virginia and establishing this Absurd Health and Holistic Medicine in 1991.

In his spare time, Dr. Hallock enjoys geocaching, pina coladas in the rain, and short walks on rocky lakesides.


Dr. Gael Rakotovao

Dr. Rakotovao is an inovating and award-wining physician for his work around the world in developing new rapid diagnostic tests to swiftly and accurately diagnose rare medical conditions, including those that have been seen in only a few locations across the globe. Additionaly as graduate of the prestigious School of Clinical medicine at the University of Cambridge, Dr. Rakotovao is considered one of the top physicians in the world.

On his days off, Dr. Rakotovao enjoys tinkering with rare and deadly viruses. He also likes to collect stamps and wine bottle corks.


Nurse Practitioner Florence Nightingale

As the founder of modern day nursing, Nurse Nightingale is a pioneer of the medical field. Since founding the Nightingale School for Nurses in 1860 (now the Florence Nightingale Faculty of Nursing and Midwifery at King's College London), she has continued to push for a revolution in medicine to more accurately diagnose and treat everyone, even those with the most absurd medical conditions.
